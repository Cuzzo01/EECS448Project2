Minesweeper

Welcome to test group don't ignore's MineSweeper!

Pick a number for your game board size (max = 30), and the number of bombs that will be placed.

Click on Setup Game to get started.

Your objective is to isolate all the mines on your board.

You can click on any square to reveal a mine or number.

If a mine is revealed you lose the game.

If a number is revealed the number indicates how many mines there are around that square.

You must reveal all non-mine squares to win.

Left mouse button will reveal the square you are hovering over.

Right mouse button will flag the square you are hovering over.

Good luck and don't suck!
